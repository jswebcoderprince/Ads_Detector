# Business Requirements

## Overview

Ads Detector is designed to solve a common challenge faced by agencies, marketers, and lead generation professionals: identifying businesses that are actively investing in digital advertising.

The product provides an automated way to discover, analyze, organize, and export high-quality business prospects by detecting advertising technologies installed on websites.

---

# Business Problem

## Current Challenge

Finding qualified prospects manually requires visiting hundreds or thousands of websites and checking:

- Whether they run advertisements
- Which marketing platforms they use
- Whether they invest in digital marketing
- Whether they are potential customers

This process is:

- Time-consuming
- Repetitive
- Difficult to scale
- Prone to human error

---

# Business Opportunity

Businesses using advertising technologies usually indicate:

- Marketing investment
- Growth intention
- Customer acquisition focus
- Budget availability

Identifying these businesses creates opportunities for agencies and service providers.

---

# Product Goal

The primary goal of Ads Detector is to create a reliable prospect intelligence system that helps users discover valuable business opportunities faster.

The product should reduce manual research time and improve lead qualification quality.

# Core Business Requirements

---

# BR-001: Automated Prospect Discovery

## Requirement

The system must allow users to upload large website lists and automatically identify potential prospects.

## Business Value

Reduces manual website research and increases lead generation capacity.

---

# BR-002: Advertising Technology Intelligence

## Requirement

The system must detect marketing technologies installed on websites.

Supported technologies:

- Google Ads
- Meta Pixel
- Microsoft UET
- TikTok Pixel
- LinkedIn Insight
- Pinterest Tag

## Business Value

Helps users identify businesses actively investing in digital marketing.

---

# BR-003: Prospect Qualification

## Requirement

The system must provide additional intelligence about detected websites.

Including:

- Confidence score
- Detection reason
- Detection method
- Prospect score

## Business Value

Allows users to prioritize better opportunities.

---

# BR-004: Data Organization

## Requirement

The system must allow users to organize prospects.

Features:

- Star prospects
- Add notes
- Add tags
- Search
- Filter

## Business Value

Creates a simple prospect management workflow without external tools.

---

# BR-005: Efficient Export

## Requirement

Users must be able to export specific prospect groups.

Export options:

- All results
- Filtered results
- Selected results
- Starred results

## Business Value

Allows users to directly use qualified leads for outreach campaigns.

---

# BR-006: Local-First Operation

## Requirement

The application should run locally without requiring cloud infrastructure.

## Business Value

Benefits:

- No monthly server cost
- Better privacy
- Unlimited scanning capacity
- Easier deployment

---

# BR-007: Historical Data Management

## Requirement

The application should store previous scans and results.

Stored data:

- Scan history
- Results
- Settings
- Notes
- Tags

## Business Value

Allows users to track previous research and avoid repeated work.

---


## Goal 3: Reduce Manual Work

# Non-Business Goals

Ads Detector is not intended to:

- Replace CRM platforms
- Run advertising campaigns
- Guarantee active ad spending
- Provide financial information
- Scrape private business data
